Shots

After that ordeal you really need a drink and you finally find the bar that Greg has been looking for another half mile down the street. Time for some shots.

The barman pours you out a selection of shots including 3 chilli, 2 baked bean, 1 bacon and 1 cherry flavoured shots. He then gives you a riddle to find the shot you want.

First, however slyly the chilli tries to hide
You will always find some on baked bean's left side;
Second, different are those who stand at either end,
But if you want cherry, neither is your friend;
Third, as you see clearly, all are different size,
Neither dwarf nor giant holds spice in their insides;
Fourth, the second left and the second on the right
Are twins once you taste them, though different at first sight.

Cherry sounds like the best option. Can you find it?
