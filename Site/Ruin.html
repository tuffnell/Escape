Szimpla Kert

It nearly broke your spirits but you finally managed to escape the white room. To celebrate you head to the nearest ruin bar, hoping to find a yourself a drink.

You go to a few different ruin bars but end up in Szimpla Kert. You get your drinks and begin to explore the nooks and cranies rooms around the bar. During your explorations you find a scrap of paper on the floor. The bartender says that it's part of a promotion and if you can solve the puzzle then you will win their "mystery prize".

Can you solve the puzzle and win the "mystery prize"?
